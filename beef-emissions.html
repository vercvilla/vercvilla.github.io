Beef Emissions Poster

COURSE

Typography!

Yale University, Fall 2019

ASSIGNMENT

Design a poster around a cause. Must be in black and white and only use typographic elements.

DESCRIPTION

I created a poster to raise awareness about emissions generated through beef production. The words, BEEF, POULTRY, and POTATOES "float" like greenhouse gasses. The letters in BEEF are 13 times larger than those in POULTRY and 57 times larger than those in POTATOES, which reflects the relative carbon footprint of each product.
