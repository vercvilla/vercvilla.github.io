Invisible Planets

COURSE

Typography: Expression, Structure, and Sequence

Yale University, Spring 2020

ASSIGNMENT

Design a book for text of your own choice.

DESCRIPTION

“Invisible Planets” is the title of a Chinese short story about different imaginary planets and their unique characteristics. I designed a book for the story and its English translation primarily for English readers. The typography of each anecdote varies according to the place being described. For example, I strike through the text of the first anecdote about a planet whose natives are notorious for their constant lying. The blue strips on the edges are reminiscent of tabs in a travel guide. Words and images in the book are in blue to add a sense of otherworldliness to them. Every place described in "Invisible Planets" reflects an aspect of our own world, and so I also included pictures taken on my college campus of strange textures in familiar places. In both the photographs and the story, what seems alien is actually quite close to home.
