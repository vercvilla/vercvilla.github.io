The Yale Globalist Magazine, Control Issue

ABOUT

The Yale Globalist is an undergraduate magazine of international affairs. Each issue centers around a theme, such as "Empathy" or "Control."

MY ROLE

I was the Creative Director of The Yale Globalist, an undergraduate magazine of international affairs, from May 2019 to April 2020. I oversaw the production of all Globalist magazines during the 2019-2020 school year, including this issue, "Control." I trained and led a 5-person design team and worked with my designers to layout issues of the publication.
